Say What?!

Say What is a party game I’ve designed and developed from the ground up. It’s been my way of exploring all fronts of the app-creating process. My main inspiration for the user experience was taken from popular pain points found in reviews of various other party game apps. Needless to say, it wasn’t a walk in the park. But man, was it fun to make.

Personal Achievements

  • Designed with custom animations and UI Components
  • Integrated popular libraries: Firebase, Google Play, Lottie, Butterknife

Takeaways

  • Truly user-centered design needs consistent user feedback
  • Early marketing and media production helps focus development efforts
