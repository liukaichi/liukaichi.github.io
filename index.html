BandLab
Case Study

I had a great time analyzing the music recording app, BandLab. Because this app is already well-established, I knew whatever changes I made had to accommodate millions of existing users. The biggest pain points I personally encountered was navigation. So I spent a couple days interviewing that verified similar experiences to mine. So I made some navigation tweaks based on some simple user personas I made.

Personal Achievements

  • Users navigated up to 88% faster for multiple personas, with no mistakes
  • Drastic improvements with minimal changes to help both new and veteran users

Takeaways

  • Having each page of the app helps in testing, even if those pages aren't being tested.
  • You don't always need a TON of iteration -- Do what works, and be willing to switch it up if it doesn't

TempBLE

Xamarin Developer | UI/UX Designer

Tecnosoft engaged me about an app mainly for truck drivers who need to monitor their temperature-sensitive goods, like frozen food. In the spirit of cold things (and to match their logo color), I decided to go with a fully blue UI. This helps accentuates the red warnings so drivers know something’s wrong without taking their eyes off the road. Features include Bluetooth Low Energy pairing, PDF export, and realtime temperature graphing.

Personal Achievements

  • First time using Xamarin to make a full-fledged app
  • Used UX concepts to collaborate a better direction for the app

Takeaways

  • Proxies are very valueable for UI testing and dummy data
  • Consistent, effective communcation between all parties is crucial to success
